# Current Paper Design Revision

> Date: 2026-06-05  
> Scope: design revision only. No empirical results were added.

## 1. Revised Position

The paper is now designed as:

> **Domain-validity-gated MR identification and executable test assets for SciML.**

The MeshGraphNets cylinder-flow material is the case study, not the main conceptual novelty.

## 2. Route-Map Placement

This paper follows three upstream works:

1. scientific-computing MR hierarchical classification/topology model;
2. NOETHER meta-pattern and semantic-variant work;
3. hierarchy-relative minimal-complete MR selection theory.

It fills the execution layer:

> candidate MR -> domain-validity rubric -> MR card -> executable asset -> relation-level verdict ledger.

## 3. Contribution Order

The revised contribution order is:

1. domain-validity rubric for candidate SciML MRs;
2. MR-card and executable-asset workflow;
3. relation-level verdict and ledger scheme;
4. MeshGraphNets cylinder-flow case study and MetBench-style reproducible assets.

NOETHER is now treated only as one candidate-source organization aid. It is not the contribution evaluated in this paper.

## 4. Blocked Claims

The paper must still not claim:

- first physics-based MT for learned fluid or physical-field predictors;
- MR superiority over rollout accuracy;
- SUT reliability or unreliability;
- pass/fail rates or violation rates;
- validated failure localization;
- broad generalization to all SciML surrogates.

These claims require SUT runs, verdict ledgers, baselines, and, for localization, seeded faults or known failure-layer evidence.
